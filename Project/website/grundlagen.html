Comparison and Logical Operators

Here are the comparison and logical operators represented using Unicode entities:

  • Negation: &#x00AC; (¬)
  • Conjunction (AND): &#x2227; (∧)
  • Disjunction (OR): &#x2228; (∨)
  • Exclusive OR: &#x22BB; (⊻)
  • Equality: &#x003D; (=)
  • Inequality: &#x2260; (≠)
  • Less than or equal: &#x2264; (≤)
  • Greater than or equal: &#x2265; (≥)
  • Implication: &#x2192; (→)
  • Equivalence: &#x2194; (↔)

Source: These Unicode character codes can be found in the official Unicode standard at Unicode Charts.
